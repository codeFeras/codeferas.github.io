More About Me


Hello! I'm Feras, an aspiring data scientist and recent LPU grad.

Passionate about the dynamic realm of data science, I'm fascinated by the potential to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets while also cultivating interests in coding videogames, and teaching / tutoring (both coding and non-coding things!).

Throughout my academic journey, I've actively engaged in projects spanning credit card fraud detection, counterfeit product identification, and sales data analysis. Notably, I contributed to impactful projects like 'Truth Guard' during the 'Kavach 2023' hackathon by the Indian Government, and actively participated in various hackathons, including 'DeveloperWeek 2024 Hackathon,' 'HackEMinds 2024,' and 'GitLab Innovation Pitch Competition. Please see my resume (or continue scrolling down here) if you want to learn more about my projects.

Above all, I am passionate about altruistic causes using technology. For example, I've also volunteered with NGOs, supplying resources to those in need and tutoring middle and high school students in villages. Spreading awareness about opportunities in programming is a cause I'm deeply committed to.

In my free time, I explore the nuances of data science, enjoy puzzle hunts, and stay committed to fitness. Excited about the prospects in data science, I'm dedicated to contributing my analytical skills and fostering ongoing learning in a forward-thinking team and traveling :)
